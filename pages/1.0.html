THE Belt and Road


The Belt and Road simulation contest is an educational activity. In this activity, participants will be assigned different roles, using their knowledge and skills, strategies and solutions for better cooperation and negotiation The Belt and Road.

All cases were based on the area of competition in the real world the way issues, all cases shall be made by the local government, public and private sector representatives, non government organizations and transnational corporations to work together to complete.

The purpose of the competition is to educate participants, so that they gain experience and knowledge, better strategy, promote the The Belt and Road related project implementation. This requires the participants to have a wealth of legal and international related knowledge, knowledge of the process of globalization and local culture, as well as related skills such as data collection, public speaking, debate, writing, creative and critical thinking, teamwork, leadership, and so on.

Silk Road Economic Belt twenty-first Century Maritime Silk Road, also known as The Belt and Road plan (OBOR), or with the road plan (BRI), is advocated by the government Chinese, connecting the Eurasian system project. The project aims to promote the economic prosperity of the participating countries, the economic cooperation of the countries in the region and the understanding and mutual trust between different civilizations, and ultimately the peace and development of the world.

The Belt and Road plan to promote Asia, Europe and Africa, the connection between. The Belt and Road plans to follow the Charter of the United Nations, to support the peaceful development of the five principles of mutual respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity, non aggression, non-interference in each other's internal affairs, equality and mutual benefit and peaceful coexistence.

The Silk Road Economic Belt connects China with the Persian Gulf and Mediterranean Sea through Central Asia and Western Asia, and connects China to Southeast Asia, South Asia and India ocean at sea. In twenty-first Century, the target of the maritime silk road was to proceed from the coast of China, through the South China Sea, the India ocean and the South Pacific, and eventually China and Europe were joined as a route. The Belt and Road Chinese plan will have the opportunity to use the comparative advantage of broad area, promote the economic openness.
